Popular Trips

Ucayali River Journey

Explore the wild beauty of the Ucayali River, paddling through dense rainforest and spotting diverse wildlife. This expedition includes stops at remote riverbanks for hiking and cultural exchanges with local communities.

Rio Negro Expedition

Venture into the mysterious waters of the Rio Negro, navigating its unique black waters and encountering rare wildlife. This expedition includes night rafting experiences, jungle hikes, and visits to indigenous villages to learn about local traditions.

Madeira River Adventure

Experience the majestic Madeira River, with its varied rapids and serene stretches. This journey offers opportunities to discover hidden waterfalls, fish for local species, and camp under the stars along the riverbanks.
